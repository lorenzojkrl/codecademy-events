Maneskin
Måneskin (Italian: [ˈmɔːneskin],[1] Danish: [ˈmɔːnəˌske̝nˀ];[2] Danish for 'moonlight') is an Italian rock band formed in Rome in 2016. The group's line-up consists of vocalist Damiano David, bassist Victoria De Angelis, guitarist Thomas Raggi and drummer Ethan Torchio.[3] Performing in the streets in their early days, the band rose to prominence after finishing second in the eleventh season of the Italian talent show X Factor in 2017, and international prominence when they won the Sanremo Music Festival 2021 and the Eurovision Song Contest 2021 – as the Italian representatives in the latter – with the song "Zitti e buoni".
